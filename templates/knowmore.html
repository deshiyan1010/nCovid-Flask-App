How to use?

  • Feature 1:
    Predictive Graph
    Select the region. Type of graph (cumilative or daily). Category of the graph(Confirmed, Recovered or Deceased). Then select a date till where you would like to predict the number of cases. Press "Graph It!" to plot the graph.
  • Feature 2:
    Statewise Data
    This feature is used to give districtwise statistics for a selected state.
  • Feature 3:
    Zone Status
    This feature is used to check the status of the district for the selected state. The zones may be red, orange or green.
  • Feature 4:
    Resources And Essentials
    This feature lists out the resources and essentials that one can avail ones need. The name of service, the contact number, the category of service are mentioned in the above table.
  • Feature 5:
    News
    Here all the latest news about Covid-19 will be available which is a clickable text to redirect to the news article.
